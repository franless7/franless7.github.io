Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Smoke app ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

Smoke does not require you to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

All user-related data (such as daily cigarette count, preferences, and progress) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

The app may display embedded YouTube videos for educational and motivational purposes. YouTube may collect data according to its own Privacy Policy.

The app also uses Lottie animations for visual presentation. Lottie does not collect or transmit personal data.

Please review YouTube's Privacy Policy for more information:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to third parties.

Data Security

All data remains on your device. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Smoke is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
