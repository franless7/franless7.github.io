Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 12, 2025

Thank you for using FootballQuizApp. This privacy policy explains how we handle information within the app.

FootballQuizApp does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The app does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any identifiable information.

2. Third-Party Frameworks

The app uses the following third-party frameworks:

3. Internet Access

The app may access the internet only to retrieve publicly available image assets related to quiz content. No analytics, tracking, or advertising data is transmitted.
