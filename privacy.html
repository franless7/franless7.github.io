Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 12, 2025

Thank you for using FootballQuizApp. This privacy policy explains how we handle information within the app.

1. Data Collection and Usage

FootballQuizApp **does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable data** from users. The app does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information.

1.1 Game Center

The app utilizes **Apple's Game Center** for leaderboards and achievements to enhance the gaming experience. Game Center processes **non-personally identifiable data** such as high scores, progress, and user nicknames as outlined in Apple's privacy policy. **We do not access or store your Game Center account data.**

1.2 Log Data and Analytics

We use standard analytics and crash reporting services (such as those provided by Apple) that automatically record certain information ("Log Data") whenever you use the App. This Log Data may include information such as your device IP address, device type, operating system version, the time and date of your use, and other statistics used **solely for bug fixing and improving the user experience.**

1.3 Advertising

As stated in the original document, **No analytics, tracking, or advertising data is transmitted** by the app itself. If we decide to introduce advertising in the future, this policy will be immediately updated to detail the third-party ad networks used and their data collection practices.

2. Third-Party Frameworks

The app uses the following third-party frameworks:

  • Kingfisher — used solely for loading and caching images from the internet. No user data is transmitted to the developer or third parties.
  • Lottie — used to display local animation files (JSON). It does not collect or transmit user data.

3. Internet Access

The app may access the internet only to retrieve publicly available image assets related to quiz content.

4. Children’s Privacy

FootballQuizApp is suitable for all ages and does not contain content inappropriate for children.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date above. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: footballquizapp@gmail.com

By using FootballQuizApp, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
